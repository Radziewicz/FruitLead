Fruitlead

We are a dynamic, fast growing company specialized in wholesale of fresh and frozen fruits and vegetables. We work with the best producers around the world, offering the best quality products. Our customers are the largest companies in the agri-food industry: supermarkets, warehouses and processing plants.

Our goal is to offer the highest quality products, at attractive prices, optimally suited to the customers' requirements! In everyday work, we rely on values ​​such as openness, cooperation and trust, which are the basis for building our relationships between colleagues, clients and suppliers and a key element of sustainable development.

We also offer comprehensive packaging, logistics and transport services (by land, sea and air). We have experience in domestic and international transport, including Europe, Asia or Africa, also with shipping containers or other types of cargo.

Cooperation

Manufacturer

We invite you to cooperation if you are a farmer or agricultural producer who is looking for reliable, long-term partner. We will help you sell your crops because we believe that every product can find a suitable buyer if all parties are open and honest. We always take care of all the details of the transaction because we know that satisfaction of our suppliers and customers is the crucial thing in our business.

Wholesale

If you sell fruit and vegetables on wholesale markets fell free to ask us for special offer! We will be happy to prepare for you the best price offer of fresh, frozen or dried products that will meet your needs, regardless of the scale or experience of your business. Our range is very wide, both in terms of quantity and the quality of our products.

Supermarket

We work with the largest supermarket chains in Poland and in Europe. Among others we are a regular supplier to the Carrefour Poland. Our products meet all the required quality standards, have the lowest price, but also a wide range of available packaging forms and flexibility of logistics.

Processing plant

We are pleased to coopearte with production and processing plants of various types of fresh, dried and frozen fruits in whole Europe. We offer products from many countries, which helps to protect our customers against price or quality fluctuations, related to the season, weather or fashion. Our prices are very competitive and the quality is strictly adapted to the requirements of a given customer.
